Education


  • Greenville High School

    Greenville High School is a small school in rural Greenville, Maine with an enrollment of about 90 students. With such a low teacher-student ratio the students are held very accountable by their educatiors. Greenville excels in comparison to it's surrounding schools. Although it is in the poorest county in the state of Maine, the small school still manages to place among the top 10% in the state, competing with much larger and wealthier schools.

  • University of Maine

    The University of Maine is the state's flagship university and state school. With a current undergraduate enrollment of about 10,000, the University provides nearly 100 majors, minors, and academic programs. The University of Maine holds students from 49 different states, and 67 countries. It is a place of education, student life, and exploration!
